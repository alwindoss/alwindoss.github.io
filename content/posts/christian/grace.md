What is grace?

![Image](img/grace.jpg)

Most of us would answer its the blessing that God gives us, it is the power that God pours into our hearts, its the healing that God does and we have so many other definitions for grace.

In Ephesians 3:8 we have Paul saying "Although I am less than the least of all God's people, this grace was given me: to preach to the gentiles..."
It is very clear from this scripture that God does not give us grace to preach the gospel instead it is a grace that God is asking us to preach the Gospel.

As unworthy as we are we do not deserve to know the secrets of the kingdom but God in His infinite love chooses to reveal to us these secrets that He had hidden in Him through out, "To them God has chosen to make known among the gentiles the glorious riches of this mystery, which is Christ in you, the hope of glory" Col 1:27

This is grace that a mystery so deep and enriching was revealed to sinners whom God chose to save through His son Jesus.

We often run after blessings seeking the hand of God. I am not saying it is wrong. All I am saying is we are looking in the wrong direction all together. It is Jesus that we need to seek, Jesus in us, the Hope of Glory. It is Him that we need to seek. We need to seek His face not His hand.

When I say "Seek His face" I mean to say Ask Jesus to reveal who He is to you. Am sure when He starts doing that our life will change. No failure can pull you down, no discouragement can be so discouraging because We have found life, life in all its fullness: The hope of glory
